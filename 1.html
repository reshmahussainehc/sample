ADMISSION PROCEDURE

  • All admissions into the College are made as per the guidelines of Bharathiar University and Tamil Nadu State Government.
  • Application for admission into the College should be submitted only in the prescribed form available in the college office.
  • Normally, admissions are done only once in the beginning of the academic year.
  • Details regarding eligibility for admission, documents to be submitted, fees to be paid etc., are given in the prospectus issued along with the Application form.
  • Any candidate who is found to have obtained admission by false representation will be summarily dismissed in future.
  • If the student is absent without a valid reason and leave letter for more than five days continuously from the date of admission or reopening of the College whichever is later, the studentâ€™s name will be removed from the register.
  • If a student leaves the College in the middle of any semester, she cannot claim remission of any portion of the fees.
  • Students who have completed their course of studies, can get their Transfer and Conduct Certificates after clearing the dues to the Laboratories and Library submitting written application to the Principal.
  • If a student leaves the College at the end of an academic year, she can receive the Attendance Certificate required by the University if she applies for it.
